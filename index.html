CSAG

CSAG Systems Administrator Guild
Founded in 2017 by Taegan Brannon and Mitchell Royer,
the group is a friendly, international network of sysadmins who are fond of recursive acronyms.

At the moment, the group's main efforts are focused on eco.os, a gentoo-based minimal-resource linux distro for the raspberry pi zero.
If you would like to contribute, feel free to contact us via Telegram (preferred), GitHub, or Email (all linked below).
